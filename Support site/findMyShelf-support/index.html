About FindMyShelf

FindMyShelf lets you take a photo of a supermarket aisle sign and automatically extract the aisle information. After choosing a store, you can search for a product (like “milk” or “rice”) and the app suggests which aisle(s) it should be in — even if the aisle text isn’t an exact match.

Store and aisle data is shared so your improvements can help other shoppers too.
